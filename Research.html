How to Start Research as a Beginner with TGRP

The Good Research Project focuses on completing real research and guiding you through journal submission. Publication is aimed for, but never guaranteed.

The fastest growth path is a Mixed Combo of courses:

  1. Start with Introduction to Research & Publication.
  2. Write a short Letter to the Editor (1 week).
  3. Move on to an Original Research or Narrative Review.
  4. Tackle advanced work like a Network Meta-analysis.

Platform-specific Advice

  • USMLE: Matched IMGs held ≈ 8 publications in 2024. Aim to meet or exceed this. Download brochure.
  • UK (IMT): First/corresponding author in PubMed-indexed Original Research scores highest—consider Original Research or Combo 2 OR.
  • AMC (Australia): No strict count, but a balanced CV helps—Mixed Combo 3 is ideal.
