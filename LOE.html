Frequently Asked Questions

Are publication fees included?

No. Journals may charge $0–50; fees are paid directly by students.

Time commitment?

One 1-hour session plus ~5 h of writing; most finish in 3-5 days.

Beginner friendly?

Yes—over half our learners are beginners. Doing the Intro course first is helpful.

What if the journal rejects the article?

We resubmit to the next suitable indexed journal until acceptance.

Any patients involved?

No—this is desk research only.

Who teaches?

Dr Shreya Deoghare (MBBS, MD-DVL) and the TGRP mentor team.
