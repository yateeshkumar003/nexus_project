About The Founder

The Good Research Project (TGRP), a pioneering initiative in medical research education, was established in May 2022 by Dr. Shreya Deoghare, MBBS, MD DVL (dermatology), our dedicated founder and CEO.

Connect with Dr. Deoghare: LinkedIn | ResearchGate | Twitter

The Problem

Medical education in India primarily revolves around diagnosing and treating diseases, leaving practicing doctors with limited time for research and publication. The instruction on medical research is primarily theoretical, lacking practical components. In this context, medical students interested in research face challenges in finding mentors to guide them through the process.
