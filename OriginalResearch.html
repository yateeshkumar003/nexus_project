Program Outline

A cohort of 4-6 students completes an observational study with the goal of publishing an Original Research article in a PubMed-indexed journal.

Key Points

  • New batches : 5th & 20th each month.
  • Announcements : Instagram & WhatsApp.
  • Fees : Displayed in INR; local currency shown at checkout.
  • Batch size : 4-6 students • Duration : 2-3 weeks.

What happens after registration?

  1. Payment confirmation email (instantly).
  2. Topic/specialty selection email (within 1-2 days).
  3. Join project group via link provided.
  4. Intro session → data-collection tasks with deadlines.
  5. Statistics session → interpretation training.
  6. Writing session → article drafting & editing.
  7. Journal shortlist → submission & peer-review support.
  8. Bonus : learn to write per STROBE guidelines.

Authorship Policy

We strictly follow ICMJE criteria. To qualify, a student must:

  1. Contribute substantially to study design, data acquisition/analysis.
  2. Draft or critically review the manuscript.
  3. Approve the final version.
  4. Accept accountability for all aspects of the work.

Authorship order is based on total contribution—tasks are allocated first-come, first-served.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is this a paid course?

Fees cover mentor guidance, statistician time, coordinators, editing, plagiarism checks, and submission support.

Are publication fees included?

No. Journal fees (if any) are paid directly by students. We guide you toward affordable indexed journals.

Time commitment & duration?

Projects need about 6-8 hours total over 2-3 weeks.

I’m a beginner—can I join?

Yes—over half our students are beginners. We recommend taking our Intro course first.

What if a journal rejects our article?

We resubmit to the next suitable indexed journal until it is published.

Ethics approval?

Most projects are virtual surveys; ethics exemption is secured via our partner committee.
