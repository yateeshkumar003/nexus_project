Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Systematic Review, Meta-analysis, and Network Meta-analysis?

A systematic review summarizes all relevant studies on a question. A meta-analysis pools their data statistically. A network meta-analysis (NMA) compares multiple interventions simultaneously—even without direct head-to-head trials—ranking treatments by effectiveness and safety.

Q1 · How do I register and what happens after registration?

Pay the fee using the link above. After payment, you’ll receive app-download instructions and be added to the project group within 24 h.

Q2 · How is authorship decided?

Authorship relies on contribution: complete ≥ 20 % of assigned tasks. Scores determine order; highest contributor is first author.

No journal can be guaranteed, but full support continues until acceptance if you stay active.

Q6 · Typical publication charges?

$0-$3,000 depending on journal. Mentors guide you toward low-fee or waiver options.

Q7 · I’m a beginner—should I join?

NMA is advanced. New researchers should first complete our Intro, Case Report, or Narrative Review courses.
