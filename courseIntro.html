Introduction to Research & Publication

Overview
What after you register?

Overview

A one-hour brain-storming video session for beginners that provides a practical understanding of the methods involved in conducting research and publishing its findings.

What is included:

  • Types of research
  • The process/steps of conducting research
  • Ethical committee approval – what it is and when it is needed
  • Plagiarism: how to assess and remove it
  • Why is it difficult to publish? Why is such a long time required?
  • What is peer review?
  • Why do journals charge fees?
  • How to identify predatory journals and avoid publication in them
  • How to make your research visible to other researchers
  • Abstracting / Indexing / DOI
  • Importance of having ORCID ID, ResearchGate, LinkedIn and Google Scholar
  • How to use Good Research Project to enhance your CV
