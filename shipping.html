Shipping Policy

The Good Research Project primarily provides digital/virtual mentorship services. Most deliverables (sessions, feedback, templates) are shared electronically. Where physical materials are involved, the following policy applies.

1. Service Nature

Our offerings are predominantly digital. No physical shipping is required for typical mentorship activities unless explicitly agreed.

2. Dispatch Timelines (If Applicable)

  • Physical items (e.g., certificates, printed materials) are dispatched within 5–10 business days after confirmation.
  • Custom or bulk print requests may require additional time; timelines will be communicated in advance.

3. Delivery Partners & Coverage

We use reputable courier/postal partners for domestic deliveries within India. International deliveries may be considered case-by-case with additional charges.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping fees, if applicable, will be communicated before order confirmation and included in the invoice. Any customs or import duties (for international shipments) are borne by the recipient.

5. Tracking & Notifications

For shipped items, tracking details will be shared via email/WhatsApp where available. Delivery times depend on the courier and destination.

6. Address Accuracy

Customers are responsible for providing accurate and complete delivery addresses. Re-delivery due to an incorrect address may incur additional charges.

7. Delays & Force Majeure

Delays caused by courier disruptions, weather, strikes, or other force majeure events are outside our control. We will assist with follow-ups to the extent possible.

8. Damaged or Lost Shipments

If a shipment arrives damaged or is lost in transit, report it to us within 3 business days with photos/evidence. We will coordinate with the courier for resolution or replacement where applicable.

9. Contact

For shipping-related queries, contact: info@thegoodresearchproject.com or +91-90752 31153.
